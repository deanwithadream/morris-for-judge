About Kerry

A Lifetime of Service to New Mexico

Kerry J. Morris has been a proud New Mexican since 1953. His career in law spans over four and a half decades — beginning as a prosecutor and continuing in private practice representing individuals and small businesses throughout New Mexico.

A graduate of UNM School of Law (1981), Kerry brings the experience, temperament, and integrity that Metropolitan Court Division 5 deserves.

Kerry is proudly endorsed by the Albuquerque Police Officers Association. He is joined in this campaign by his wife Gloria and son Andrew.

Platform

Fair, Impartial, & Independent

Kerry believes in a judiciary that serves every person equally — regardless of politics, power, or circumstance.

📜

Uphold the Constitution

Courts must uphold the Constitution and Bill of Rights as written — protecting the liberties of every New Mexican.

⚖️

Adjudicate, Don't Legislate

Judges should decide the cases before them — not make policy from the bench. That role belongs to elected lawmakers.

🤝

Equal Justice for All

Every party deserves to be treated equally under the law, free from political or personal considerations.
